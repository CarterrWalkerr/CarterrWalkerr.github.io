About Me

Where I'm From

Born and raised in Queen Creek, Arizona, I moved to Orem, Utah in high school — and ended up staying for BYU. The desert to the mountains was a good trade.

Mission

I served a two-year mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Lisbon, Portugal. It changed how I see people, hard work, and the world. I came back fluent in Portuguese.

What Drives Me

I love spontaneity — saying yes before I have a plan, trying things I've never done, and making the most out of every day. Whether it's a new trail, a road trip, or a pickup basketball game, I'm in.

People

My people are everything. My girlfriend Paris, my teammates, my family, my friends — I care most about the people around me and the memories we make together.
