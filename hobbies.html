Hobbies

What I do when I'm not in class or at practice

Basketball

Basketball is my go-to. Whether it's a pickup game at the gym or a late-night run with friends, I'll never say no to a game. It's one of the best ways I know to compete, have fun, and connect with people — all at once.

🏀

Pickup Games

Always down for a run. I'll show up to any gym, any time.

⚡

Competitive

I play to win. Track has trained me to give everything in short bursts.

🤝

Social

Half the reason I play is the people. Best conversations happen on the court.

My Favorite Things About the Game

  1. The competitiveness — every possession matters
  2. You can play anywhere, with anyone, at any time
  3. It rewards athleticism and IQ equally
  4. Nothing beats a buzzer beater with friends watching
  5. The trash talk (respectful, of course)

Track & Field

I'm a D1 Track & Field athlete at BYU. Track has taken me to places I never expected — different states, different campuses, different people. Competing at this level has taught me more about discipline and pushing limits than anything else I've done.

Training

Early mornings, long practices, and a team that keeps you accountable every single day.

Travel

Competing means road trips and flights to meets across the country — one of the best perks.

Team Culture

Track teammates become family. You grind together, hurt together, and celebrate together.

Mental Toughness

Racing teaches you to be comfortable being uncomfortable — a skill that transfers everywhere.

Best Parts of Being a Student-Athlete

  • Representing BYU on the track
  • Traveling to meets around the country
  • The discipline carries over into everything else
  • Being part of something bigger than yourself

Adventures & Friends

If I'm not at practice or in class, I'm probably trying to talk someone into doing something spontaneous. Road trips, hiking, new restaurants, late night drives — I love making the most out of every day. My girlfriend Paris and my friends are always down for the next thing.

🗺️

New Experiences
